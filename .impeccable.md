## Design Context

### Users
**Primary audience: General readers and subscribers.** They discover, consume, and share content on the public-facing blog. They care about fast page loads, beautiful typography, effortless reading, and content discovery (tags, related posts, feeds). They are not power users — the interface must feel immediately familiar and frictionless.

Secondary users are the **content creators and org admins** who live in the admin dashboard — they care about a productive, distraction-free editor and clear publishing workflows.

**Job to be done:**
- Readers: Find something worth reading, read it without friction, discover more.
- Authors: Write and publish without fighting the tool.
- Org admins: Manage team, track performance, configure the platform.

---

### Brand Personality
**Bold · Modern · Dynamic**

The platform should feel alive — like Hashnode at its best. Strong accent colors, confident type, motion that gives feedback. Not aggressive or chaotic, but never timid. The navy blue dark theme is the signature aesthetic: deep, rich backgrounds with electric carolina-blue accents and teal gradient moments.

**Emotional goals:**
- Readers feel: _immersed, engaged, like they found something worth their time_
- Authors feel: _capable, focused, like the tool works for them_
- Admins feel: _in control, informed, nothing is hidden_

---

### Aesthetic Direction

**Primary theme: Dark-first (navy blue)**
The navy blue dark palette is the hero experience. Light mode is secondary.

**Palette anchors:**
| Role | Value | Notes |
|------|-------|-------|
| Page background | `hsl(222, 47%, 11%)` — Oxford Blue 2 | Deep, rich, not pure black |
| Card/surface | `hsl(222, 44%, 13%)` — Oxford Blue | Slightly lifted from background |
| Section/muted | `hsl(216, 33%, 20%)` — Prussian Blue | UI wells, sidebars, input bg |
| Primary accent | `hsl(199, 89%, 49%)` — Carolina Blue | CTAs, links, focus rings |
| Gradient (hero) | `#0ea5ea → #0bd1d1` | Headlines, featured elements |
| Body text | `hsl(216, 100%, 95%)` — Alice Blue | Primary readable text |
| Secondary text | `hsl(217, 24%, 59%)` — Shadow Blue | Article body, meta |
| Muted text | `hsl(216, 33%, 68%)` — Wild Blue Yonder | Labels, timestamps |

**Typography:**
- UI / headlines: `Plus Jakarta Sans` — modern, confident, geometric
- Article body: `Lora` — warm serif, long-form reading comfort
- Code: `Roboto Mono`
- Border radius: `1.25rem` — rounded, friendly, not sharp-edged

**References:**
- **Medium**: Editorial reading experience, clean post layout, story cards with author metadata
- **Hashnode**: Developer community energy, dark mode excellence, tag/feed system, bold CTAs

**Anti-references:**
- Avoid: Generic SaaS dashboard grey (boring, corporate, lifeless)
- Avoid: Cluttered news-site information overload
- Avoid: Pure-black dark modes (harsh, not the navy warmth we have)

---

### Design Principles

1. **Dark is the default canvas.** Every component is designed dark-first. Light mode is derived, not the source of truth. Never sacrifice the richness of the navy palette for light-mode parity.

2. **Content is the hero.** Typography and whitespace carry the design. The chrome (nav, sidebars, footers) recedes; the article or post comes forward. Lora at comfortable line-height is the reading contract.

3. **Bold where it counts, quiet everywhere else.** Carolina blue and the teal gradient are used for primary actions, highlights, and moments of emphasis — not scattered. The rest of the UI is calm, letting accents punch.

4. **Motion signals meaning.** Micro-interactions (hover transitions, skeleton pulses, badge lifts) give the UI personality and communicate state. Animations are purposeful: 0.25s ease for hover, 0.5s ease for larger transitions.

5. **Semantic tokens, never raw values.** All colors flow through the token system (`bg-background`, `text-muted-foreground`, etc.). No hardcoded hex/hsl in components. This ensures theme consistency across admin and public surfaces.
